In this app you can manage your to-do list! Here's how it works:

Type 'GUI' to open a graphic user interface!
You can add tasks to your to do list by typing: 'add'
You can remove items from your to do list by typing: 'delete'
You  can ask  the program to show your to do list by typing: 'list'
You can complete a task by typing 'complete'
